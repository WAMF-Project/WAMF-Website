title: "Why WAMF?"
---

WAMF exists because wildlife monitoring needs a different kind of interface from security monitoring.

Frigate is excellent at detecting objects, recording camera events and managing video streams. WAMF builds on that foundation by adding species identification, observation management, media archiving and a wildlife-focused web interface.

The goal is not to replace Frigate. The goal is to extend it for a different purpose.

WAMF turns camera detections into wildlife observations.
